Good Food Sources

Red meats are extremely important as just one large animal can provide more than enough high quality protein so you will have food left over for other days. Birds are also a good source of food, because if you have the right equipment, they are easy to catch and will provide enough meat for at least one meal. In many cultures, insects are considered a gourmet cuisine, but if you eat it, make sure to remove the wings, antennae, and legs from the insect. Ocean life is a great source of protein and fat, as it is easy to collect because you just have to be patient while you are collecting it.

How to Preserve Food

There are a couple of ways to extend the life of food while you are in the wilderness. You can dehydrate the food using salt to draw the moisture out, especially with meat, you can put the food in a container or something to cover it and put it in a hole in the ground, because its cold in the ground and it would make it like your fridge. Or you could also ferment the food by making a mixture of 3% salt, and 97% water.
